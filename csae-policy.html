Child Safety Standards

Last updated: 6 July 2026

Swim Buddy has zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) of any kind. This policy applies to all users of the Swim Buddy app and website, in every country where the service is available.

Age requirement

Swim Buddy is intended for adults only (18 years of age or older). Accounts found to belong to a minor are suspended or terminated on discovery.

Prohibited content and conduct

Users are strictly prohibited from using Swim Buddy to:

  • Upload, share, request, or link to child sexual abuse material (CSAM) in any form.
  • Sexualize, groom, solicit, or otherwise endanger a minor.
  • Contact or attempt to contact a minor for a sexual or exploitative purpose.
  • Use the platform's profile, bio, group, or messaging features to advertise, promote, or facilitate any of the above.

Reporting

Any user can report a profile, swim group, or message directly from the app, or by emailing us at contact@swim-buddy.com. Reports are reviewed by our team and, where CSAE is suspected, escalated to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and/or local law enforcement, as required by applicable law.

Enforcement

Accounts found to violate this policy are permanently banned and reported to the relevant authorities. We cooperate with law enforcement investigations to the fullest extent required by law.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to contact@swim-buddy.com.
